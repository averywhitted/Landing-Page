The Audition Survival Workshop

This workshop covers what actually moves the needle from submission to callback, with tools you can use immediately.

Workshops are held on Zoom and are currently limited to 10 people.

  • How to find better auditions through Actors Access, Backstage, EPAs, and reps.
  • Prep systems that stick: memorization methods, line drills, and fast scene choices.
  • Self-tape workflow: reader setup, lighting, edits, file delivery, and submission habits.
  • In-room and callback strategy: handling adjustments, nerves, and post-audition reset.
  • What you leave with: a repeatable framework, tools for handling anxiety, and live feedback on your work.

Private Coaching

Private coaching is available via Zoom for actors who want focused, individualized support between classes or ahead of an audition.

  • One-hour coaching sessions for deeper script work, choices, and self-tape support.
  • Thirty-minute audition prep sessions for quick turnaround sides and last-minute confidence.
  • Practical feedback tailored to where you are getting stuck, whether that is prep, clarity, or nerves.
  • Flexible Zoom sessions that keep the work specific, efficient, and immediately usable.
